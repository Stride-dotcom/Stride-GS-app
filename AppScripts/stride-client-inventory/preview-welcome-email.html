Getting Started
Everything you can do from
the Stride Client menu.
1
View Your Inventory
Open the Stride Client menu and choose View Active, View Released, or View All to filter your items by status. This is the fastest way to see what is currently in storage.
2
Request an Inspection
Select an item row on the Inventory tab, then go to Stride Client → Request Inspection. Our team will inspect the item and send you a report with photos and results.
3
Request a Repair Quote
On the Inventory tab, find the item that needs repair. Set the Task Type dropdown to Repair Estimate, then check the Create Task checkbox. Our team will assess the item and send you a quote for approval.
4
Schedule a Will Call / Release
Go to Stride Client → Create Will Call. Select the items to release, enter the pickup party and estimated date, and submit. We will prepare the items and send you a confirmation with a release document.
5
View Item History
Click on any item row, then go to Stride Client → View Item History. This shows all tasks, repairs, shipments, and billing history for that item in one view.
